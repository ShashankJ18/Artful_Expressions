The Alchemical Process

Conceptualization

Each piece begins with an idea sparked by natural forms, color relationships, or emotional states. I sketch, research, and gather materials that will bring the vision to life.

1

Material Alchemy

I experiment with resin formulas, pigments, and additives to create custom blends. This stage involves testing color interactions and material behaviors.

2

Layered Application

Resin is applied in carefully calculated layers, with each pour creating depth and dimension. This stage requires precision and patience as the material cures.

3

Revelation

After full curing, the piece is carefully extracted from its mold and finished. This is the moment of revelation when the final artwork is fully revealed.
